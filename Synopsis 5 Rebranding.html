✦ Behind The "Re-branding Kecap Benteng | LKS 2024 Graphic Design Technology Competition" ✦

It was an honor for me to win first place in the project which is a LKS SMK 2024 competition in the field of graphic design
technology at the South Tangerang city level held last February. The task was to create a re-branding of the trademark
“Kecap Benteng” which includes concept creation, logo sketching, logo vectorization, branding guideline creation,
supergraphic, packaging creation to promotional media.
