✦ Behind The "An Apple" Digital Painting ✦

In accordance with the title of this work “an apple” that has been eaten and leaves a little part of the red apple.
This work illustrates that beauty comes when we can see things for what they are. The direction of light in this picture also
supports this apple to be the main object, where the lighting comes from the side highlighting an apple on a black background
standing on a table with a remaining bite that adds to the dramatic impression of the work.
