✦ Behind The "Birthday Bass and The A's" Design ✦

This work is a modified form of a senior graphic designer named Massimo Vigneli. He emphasized that a good design is
one that can convey a message effectively. This is evidenced by his statement that “less is more”, which means that
the fewer elements displayed, the more focus on the message to be conveyed. The “fewer elements displayed” that I have
said refers to the how designer could communicate through visuals and efficient use of elements.
