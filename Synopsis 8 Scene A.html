✦ Behind The "Media Promotion Design for Scene A" ✦

Scene A studio is a production house that offers photography, videography and editing services. The company's background
led me to conceptualize a promotional media using visual elements such as tool bar to represent editing, camera lens,
clipper and drone to represent photography and videography. The selection of dominant colors such as white, yellow and
black is taken from the company's identity which is represented by the graphic logo of scene A studio. Some other graphic
utilizations such as the use of 3d elements to represent each pricelist and the use of hierarchies with unique typography
make this design able to attract attention, build trust, which will bring new clients to this production house business.
